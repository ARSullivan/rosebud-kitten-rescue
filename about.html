About Rosebud Kitten Rescue

Hi, I’m Amanda — founder of Rosebud Kitten Rescue.

My journey with kittens began when we lived in Irving, TX. A mother cat abandoned her two-week-old kittens near our home, and we simply couldn’t let them starve. My mom and I quickly gathered supplies and began bottle-feeding them. That was my first experience caring for kittens, and it sparked a lifelong calling.

After we moved to Red Oak, TX, we noticed that people were dropping off unwanted cats in our neighborhood. As a lifelong cat lover, I couldn’t turn a blind eye. I begged my mom to help feed them, and soon we had a colony of nearly 40 cats. Over time, I gained hands-on experience with kittens — from bottle-feeding to helping moms deliver litters safely.

One of the most impactful moments came with a cat named Toodles, who had epilepsy. I assisted her through a complicated birth, cutting umbilical cords and breaking water sacs, all while receiving guidance from a vet over the phone. Afterward, I raised the kittens by hand when it became clear it wasn’t safe for her to care for them.

Through the years, I’ve helped cats recover from abscesses, infections, and even major injuries — like those caused by car engines. Each challenge taught me more, thanks to the advice and support of local veterinarians. After moving away from home, I continued to help stray and feral cats and fostered for a kitten rescue, where I learned even more — including administering fluids, caring for neonates, and treating common issues like ringworm.

Now, with Rosebud Kitten Rescue, I’ve taken everything I’ve learned and created a safe, loving place for kittens and mama cats in need. My mission is to rescue, heal, and find forever homes for as many cats as possible — and to spread kindness along the way.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. 💛🐾

Although we are not yet a 501(c)(3), we are driven by passion and the belief that every kitten deserves a safe, loving home.
