About Rosebud Kitten Rescue

Rosebud Kitten Rescue is a small, independent rescue dedicated to giving vulnerable kittens and their mothers a second chance. We provide round-the-clock care, medical attention, and plenty of love until they are ready for adoption.

Although we are not yet a 501(c)(3), we are driven by passion and the belief that every kitten deserves a safe, loving home.
