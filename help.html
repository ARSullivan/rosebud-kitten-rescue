How You Can Help 🐾

Rosebud Kitten Rescue is a small single person run rescue, with a big heart and an even bigger mission: To give every kitten a chance at a safe and loving home. We rely entirely on the kindness of animal lovers like you to help us care for Kittens, Nursing Moms, Abandoned, Stray, and Feral Cats.

⚠️ Our Most Urgent Needs

  • 🛍️ Wet & dry kitten food
  • 🧴 Flea treatments for kittens and moms
  • 🪣 Kitty litter (any brand appreciated)
  • 💉 Vet care (vaccines, spay/neuter, medications)

💛 Ways You Can Support Us

  • 🍼 Donate formula, kitten food, and litter
  • 📦 Drop off supplies — message us for location info
  • 💲 Contribute via CashApp: $KittyRose8407
  • 🐈 Sponsor the care of a stray or rescue kitten
  • 🚗 Help with transportation to vet appointments

Every donation, no matter the size, helps us provide warmth, food, and medical care to kittens in need. Thank you for being part of their journey. 🐾
